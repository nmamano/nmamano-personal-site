Hello!

I am a computer scientist specialized in algorithm design. I received a PhD as part of the CS Theory group at UCI. I was fortunate to be advised by Professors David Eppstein and Michael Goodrich.

Before that, I got a bachelor's degree in CS from UPC in my hometown of Barcelona. I visited UCI as an undergrad to conduct my senior thesis under Professor Wayne Hayes.

Research

My research involves graph algorithms and computational geometry. My dissertation, New Applications of the Nearest-neighbor Chain Algorithm studies how to relax the selection rule of greedy algorithms without affecting the final solution. This idea, paired with an algorithmic technique called nearest-neighbor chain, allows us to speed up greedy algorithms for several problems such as TSP.
