Nil Mamano is a computer scientist with a PhD from UC Irvine, where his research focused on algorithms and data structures. He has co-authored nine peer-reviewed papers, with original contributions to graph algorithms, computational geometry, computational biology, and recreational mathematics. He previously worked as a senior software engineer at Google, working on scaling the networking infrastructure. Additionally, he developed the technical curriculum for coding interview prep at Pathrise.
